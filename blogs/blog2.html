How does it work?

SDXL Turbo uses a combination of a text encoder, sparse autoencoder, and a high-resolution decoder to generate images. Sparse autoencoders reduce redundant data, focusing on essential features to create a clear visual interpretation of text prompts.

Reference: Surkov, Viacheslav, et al. "Unpacking SDXL Turbo: Interpreting Text-to-Image Models with Sparse Autoencoders." arXiv preprint arXiv:2410.22366 (2024).
